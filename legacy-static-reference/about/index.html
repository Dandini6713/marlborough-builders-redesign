About us

Built on years of hands-on experience

Marlborough Builders Ltd is a family-run business built on years of hands-on trade experience.

Although the company was established in 2024, the experience behind it goes back much further, working across joinery, roofing and general building projects.

We are based in Prescot and work across Merseyside, St Helens, Warrington, Wigan and the wider Northwest for both domestic and commercial clients.
