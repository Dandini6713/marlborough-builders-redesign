Our roofing services

What our roofing team covers

Checklist of everything we take on. For how each service works in depth, see roofing services.

01

Slating

Natural slate installations and replacements for homes, heritage properties and commercial buildings.

02

Tiling

Concrete, clay and interlocking tile systems installed correctly and with the right fixing detail.

03

Re-roofs

Full strip and replacement projects approached with proper inspection and practical sequencing.

Flat roofing

Felt, EPDM and GRP systems for extensions, garages and larger commercial roof areas.

Lead work

Velux and roof lights

Repairs and emergencies

Leaks, storm damage, missing slates and urgent roof issues — stabilised and sorted quickly.

08

Guttering and fascias

Roofline upgrades, replacement guttering and associated maintenance work.
